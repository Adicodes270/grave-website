Commands

Everything GRAVE can do, in one place. Use the search or filter by category.

Moderation Security
  • /kickKick a member
  • /banBan a member
  • /timeoutTimeout a member
  • /untimeoutRemove timeout from a member
  • /unbanUnban a user by mention or ID
  • /roleAdd or remove a role from a user
  • /warnWarn a member
  • /unwarnRemove warnings from a member
  • /warnresetReset all warnings for a member
  • /warningsView warnings for a member
  • /clearwarningsClear all warnings for a member
  • /warnstatusShow a full warning status overview for a member
  • /purgeDelete the last N messages in this channel
  • /rolecreateCreate a new role by name
  • /rolesList server roles by hierarchy (paginated)
Economy Currency
  • /dailyClaim 1,000–5,000 coins once per 24 hours
  • /weeklyClaim 20,000–50,000 coins once per 7 days
  • /monthlyClaim 400,000–700,000 coins once per 30 days
  • /balanceCheck your or another member's coin balance
  • /balAlias for balance
  • /payPay another user coins (supports k/m/b/all)
  • /workWork a job to earn coins (12h cooldown). Use .work for job list
  • /bankDeposit coins to earn 1.5% daily compound interest
  • /achievementsView your achievements or another member's achievements
  • /aAlias for achievements
  • /coinflipGamble coins (supports k/m/all) on a 50/50 coin flip
  • /cfAlias for coinflip
  • /russianroullette2-player russian roulette duel (supports k/m/b/all)
  • /rrAlias for russianroullette
  • /numberhuntNumber Hunt lobby game (server-only). Public if no members; private if members given.
  • /robRob a member's coins (60% success rate)
  • /robbankRob a member's bank deposit or your own (5% success rate)
Server Setup Config
  • /welcomesetupSet the welcome channel for join messages
